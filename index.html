Their Story

From Underdogs
to Legends

BTS debuted on June 13, 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment — then a tiny company struggling to stay afloat. The seven boys came from modest backgrounds, dreaming of music in a ruthlessly competitive industry dominated by mega-agencies.

They faced years of mockery, industry gatekeeping, and near-disbandment. Albums flopped, resources were scarce, and they slept in cramped dorms. Yet they kept writing music that spoke truth — about youth, pressure, and self-worth.

When I Need U went viral in 2015, everything changed. By 2018, they stood before the United Nations speaking for the youth of the world. Their ARMY fandom built itself into a global force of love and activism.

Meet the Members

RM

Kim Nam-joon · Sept 12, 1994

Leader & Rapper

Self-taught English speaker & visual arts lover. Led BTS's UN speech. Solo album Indigo debuted globally. Donated ₩100M to National Museum of Modern & Contemporary Art.

Jin

Kim Seok-jin · Dec 4, 1992

Vocalist

Eldest member & the group's comedy heart. His solo "The Astronaut" written with Coldplay reached #1 in 17 countries. Completed mandatory military service with pride.

SUGA

Min Yoon-gi · Mar 9, 1993

Rapper & Producer

Overcame depression & a shoulder injury that nearly ended his career. As Agust D, released raw mixtapes about mental health that changed K-pop's conversation forever.

j-hope

Jung Ho-seok · Feb 18, 1994

Rapper & Dancer

First BTS member to headline Lollapalooza 2022 as a solo K-pop act. His energy holds the team together — nicknamed "Sunshine" for his relentless positivity through hardship.

Jimin

Park Ji-min · Oct 13, 1995

Vocalist & Dancer

First solo Korean artist to reach #1 on Billboard Hot 100 with "Like Crazy." Battled severe self-doubt early in career; his perseverance made him one of the most beloved performers alive.

V

Kim Tae-hyung · Dec 30, 1995

Vocalist

Passionate jazz & blues aficionado. Layover debuted #1 in 99 countries. Once hidden as a surprise trainee, he became the world's most-followed K-pop artist on Instagram.

Jungkook

Jeon Jung-kook · Sep 1, 1997

Main Vocalist · Golden Maknae

Left his hometown at 13 to pursue his dream. Youngest member and master of every art form — dance, song, art, video. "Seven" spent 6 weeks at #1 on Billboard Hot 100.

Recognition

Major Awards & Milestones

🏆

Billboard Music Awards

Won Top Social Artist 6 consecutive years (2017–2022). First Korean group to win. Also won Top Duo/Group in 2022.

🎵

Grammy Nominees

Nominated for Best Pop Duo/Group Performance at the 2021 Grammys for "Dynamite" — a historic first for a K-pop group.

🌍

UN General Assembly

Addressed world leaders twice (2018, 2021) as Presidential Special Envoys for Future Generations and Culture.

🎤

American Music Awards

First Korean act to perform at AMAs (2017). Won Artist of the Year in 2021 — one of music's most prestigious honors.

🌟

TIME 100 Most Influential

Named among TIME's 100 Most Influential People in the World multiple times, including a dedicated cover story.

💿

Record-Breaking Sales

Proof (2022) sold over 2.75M copies in its first week. They hold multiple Guinness World Records for album sales.
